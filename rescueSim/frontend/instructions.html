Welcome to RescueSim

This tool trains you to handle 911 emergency scenarios. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Choose a mode: "Text Simulation" for text-based scenarios or "Audio Simulation" for voice interactions.
  • Ask clear questions to gather details like location, emergency type, and risks.
  • Type responses in text mode or record them in audio mode.

    • --------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Alternatively, upload an audio or text file using the "Attach File" option to receive feedback.
  • After completing a session, review your performance in the "Feedback" section.

Stay calm, ask concise questions, and prioritize caller safety. For help, visit our GitHub or contact us at shritejmami@gmail.com or vandanayyalapu@gmail.com.
